ABOUT US

We believe business
can be beautiful.

We believe that doing the right thing gets results. We believe that brilliant, beautiful, commercially sustainable businesses are the future - and that this process can also be a huge amount of fun.

We naturally gravitate towards working with businesses with soul, with founders who want to do something wonderful with their business - for their team, themselves and their family. Our mission is to make it easy for people to build Beautiful Businesses.


OUR BEGINNINGS

Together, we founded Stanbooks in 2018. We believed accountants should be at the heart of building beautiful, commercially sustainable businesses.

The heartbeat of Stanbooks has always been about great people working together. Over the years, we’ve built a team of smart, caring, passionate and fundamentally nice people. A team that loves helping people achieve their dreams and being part of the exciting journey of growing a business.


WHERE WE ARE NOW

Our brilliant team helps clients to build Beautiful Businesses day after day, year after year. We're incredibly proud of the service we deliver and the impact our support has on our clients.
